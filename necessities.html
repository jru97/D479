Food & Lodging
Taniti may be a small island, but it delivers big when it comes to comfort, cuisine, and convenience. Whether you're craving local flavors, looking for a place to stay, or need to grab a few essentials, the island has you covered with just the right balance of charm and modern amenities.
Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is always open. The island also currently has ten (10) restaurants: five (5) serve local cuisine, three serve American-style meals, and two (2) serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Most restaurants and stores are located in and around Merriton Landing.

Taniti offers a variety of lodging options, from budget-friendly guesthouses to mid-range hotels. Many are located within walking distance of Merriton Landing, making it easy to explore shops, entertainment, and the bay. Most accommodations provide modern comforts while keeping that laid-back island vibe intact.

All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Reservations can be made through any major booking site or by calling +698 (504) 234-2345.
